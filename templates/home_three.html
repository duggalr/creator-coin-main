A place for Engineers to mint a ERC-721 token of themselves...

CreatorCoin serves as a medium to help individual's fund their personal side projects or research.

  • A Creator can launch a NFT for themselves, to help fund their ongoing project(s).
  • Supporters can buy the NFT, and benefit through NFT ownership.
